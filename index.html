What we do

Origin AV Studios designs, installs, and supports audio-visual systems for houses of worship, schools, corporate spaces, and live venues. We also produce professional audio, photography, and video for events and marketing.

  • Audio-Visual Integration

    End-to-end AV system design, equipment selection, installation and post-install support — including control systems and lighting.

  • Audio Engineering

    Live sound mixing, multi-track recording, timecode and sync, and in-ear monitoring solutions for touring and installed systems.

  • Photography

    Event photography, headshots, and commercial shoots delivered with professional editing and fast turnaround.

  • Videography

    Event coverage, promotional videos, interviews and post-production services including color grading and motion graphics.

Serving the Central to Eastern North Carolina regions. Available for travel to larger projects.

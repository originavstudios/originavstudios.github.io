About Origin AV Studios

Professional Audio Visual Integration • Audio Engineering • Photography • Videography

Who We Are

Origin AV Studios is a full-service audio visual and creative media company based in Havelock, North Carolina. Founded by two industry professionals with backgrounds in systems integration, live audio engineering, and digital media production, we deliver dependable and high-impact AV solutions for churches, businesses, events, and creative clients.

We believe in combining technical precision with creative excellence — ensuring every project, from an installed AV system to a cinematic video shoot, performs flawlessly and looks extraordinary.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to elevate experiences. Whether it’s designing an audio system that fills a room with clarity, engineering a mix that captures emotion, or producing visuals that tell a powerful story, we aim to create work that exceeds expectations and stands the test of time.

Experience You Can Trust

  • Years of hands-on experience in professional AV integration and live production
  • Certified training in both audio systems and camera technology
  • Past work includes churches, small businesses, musicians, non-profits, and corporate presentations
  • Reliable project management and client-focused communication
